Popular US Spartan Race Venues

Here are some of the best locations in the United States and the race types they typically offer.

  1. East Coast
    • New Jersey (Tri-State) - Sprint, Super, Beast, Ultra, and Kids races
    • Vermont - Known for tough mountain Beast and Trifecta weekends
    • North Carolina (Fayetteville) - Full range of distances
  2. West Coast & Mountains
    • California (San Diego / Pala) - Sprint, Super, and family-friendly events
    • Washington (Seattle area) - Sprint and Super trail races
    • Arizona (Phoenix) - Desert-style Sprint and Super challenges
  3. Midwest & South
    • Texas (Dallas) - Sprint and stadium-style races
    • Michigan (Grand Rapids) - Scenic trail and mud runs
